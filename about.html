About Me

Hi there! I'm Baylee Peterson, currently a student at Fort Hays State University. I'm working towards earning my Bachelor's degree in Informatics with a focus in Web Development. Alongside my degree, I'm also completing certificates in Full Stack Web Development and Marketing. My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of web development.

For over two years, I've been working as a freelance graphic designer. During this time, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, ranging from website creation to designing logos, advertisements, custom t-shirt designs, videos, and full streaming graphics. This diverse experience has honed my skills and allowed me to deliver high-quality, visually appealing, and functional designs tailored to meet my clients' needs.

Outside of my professional and academic pursuits, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, as well as traveling to find good views, and indulging in my love for music. Whether I'm enjoying the views I find or dancing my way through my favorite artist's concert, I find inspiration in the world around me. This balance between work, study, and personal interests keeps me grounded and fuels my creativity.

My passion for design and technology drives me to continuously learn and improve. I take great pride in my ability to bring my clients' visions to life, whether it's through a sleek new website or a captivating logo. I'm excited to continue growing in this field and to take on new challenges that allow me to combine my technical expertise with my creative flair.
